Our Favourite Mince Beef Recipes

Spicy Beef Burrito 1

Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 15 mins | Calories: 620 kcal | Protein: 34g

Ingredients: Mince beef, black beans, rice, red pepper, onions, tortillas, salsa

A hearty burrito loaded with seasoned beef, beans, and spiced rice — perfect for a satisfying lunch or dinner.

Method:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and brown the mince beef.
  2. Add diced onions and red pepper, cook until soft.
  3. Stir in cooked rice and black beans. Season and mix well.
  4. Warm tortillas, fill with mixture, and roll tightly.
Classic Cottage Pie

Prep: 25 mins | Cook: 30 mins | Calories: 560kcal | Protein: 32g

Ingredients: Mince beef, carrots, peas, onions, mashed potatoes, beef stock

A British classic — rich beef base topped with creamy mashed potato, baked to golden perfection.

Method:
  1. Fry mince beef until browned. Add diced onions, carrots, and peas.
  2. Pour in beef stock and simmer until vegetables are tender.
  3. Spoon into a baking dish and top with mashed potatoes.
  4. Bake at 180°C until golden and bubbling (about 20 minutes).
Chilli Con Carne

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 45 mins | Calories: 480kcal | Protein: 36g

Ingredients: Mince beef, kidney beans, bell peppers, onions, tomato paste, chilli powder

A fiery favourite made with rich mince beef, kidney beans, and bold spices — ideal served with rice or nachos.

Method:
  1. Brown mince beef in a deep pan.
  2. Add chopped onions and peppers. Cook until soft.
  3. Mix in tomato paste, kidney beans, and chilli powder.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 30–40 minutes. Stir occasionally.
Spaghetti Bolognese

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 30 mins | Calories: 540kcal | Protein: 30g

Ingredients: Mince beef, onions, garlic, carrots, chopped tomatoes, spaghetti

An Italian-inspired classic — tender beef ragu simmered with herbs and tomatoes, served over spaghetti.

Method:
  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and carrots until soft.
  2. Add beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in chopped tomatoes, season, and simmer for 20 mins.
  4. Cook spaghetti separately and serve with the sauce.
